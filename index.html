Nuno Miguel Martins Pacheco

Olá! I'm Miguel, and I design how people collaborate with intelligent systems.

As a Senior Researcher at Siemens, I focus on AI UX and product innovation, bridging human-centered research and product development. My work turns explainability, interaction design, and user insights into tangible results for Siemens' AI platforms and applications. I combine user research, prototyping, and IP creation to make AI systems more transparent, intuitive, and trustworthy.

I earned my Dr.-Ing. in Mechanical Engineering from the Technical University of Munich (2024), where I led research on hackathons for innovative mechatronic product development and managed projects like the €1.3M MCube STEAM mobility initiative showcased at IAA Mobility 2023. During my doctoral research, I led the THINK. MAKE. START. programme (2019-2024), fostering innovation by guiding students in solving real-world problems with emerging technologies.

From 2022-2024, I led design thinking and prototyping workshops at Mintech Engineering, training over 100 professionals from BMW Group, Siemens, and other industry partners in ideation, prototyping, and storytelling.

I hold an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering and Management from TU Munich and a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering and Economics from TU Graz.

Outside of work, I'm an advanced hobby barista who loves a good espresso, a vinyl enthusiast spinning classics and new hits, a passionate analog photographer, and a hobby cook exploring different cuisines. I also enjoy road/gravel biking (Munich to Bolzano) and hiking in the Alps, Norway, and Portugal.

Since 2022, I work as a Freelancer (Workshop Instructor & Concept Developer) at Mintech Engineering providing Workshops to different partners in industry. I primarily lead design thinking and prototyping workshops with cross-functional teams from idea to product concept, focusing on ideation, user need identification, prototyping, and storytelling. I successfully led workshops with multiple industry partners (e.g., BMW Group, Siemens Digital Industries, UnternehmerTUM Makerspace).

I have an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering and Management (Product Development, Production, and Innovation focus) from Technical University of Munich and have a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering and Economics from Technical University of Graz.
